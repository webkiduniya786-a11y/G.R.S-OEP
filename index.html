    Services

    At G.R.S International Overseas Employment Promoters,

    Overseas Manpower Recruitment

    We specialize in supplying skilled, semi-skilled, and unskilled workers for various sectors including construction, healthcare, hospitality, oil & gas, engineering, and maintenance — mainly for Saudi Arabia and Gulf countries.

    Visa Processing & Documentation

    Our expert team manages all documentation and visa procedures, ensuring compliance with Pakistan’s Bureau of Emigration & Overseas Employment (BEOE) regulations and the host country’s labor laws.

    Candidate Screening & Selection

    We carefully screen, interview, and select candidates according to the employer’s requirements to guarantee the right fit for every position.

    Trade Testing & Training

    Through our partner institutes, we arrange trade testing and pre-departure training to ensure workers meet international standards of skill and discipline.

    Travel & Deployment Assistance

    From air ticketing to final deployment, we handle all logistics — providing a hassle-free experience for both clients and employees.

    After-Deployment Support

    Our responsibility doesn’t end at placement. We stay connected with our clients and workers to ensure ongoing satisfaction, safety, and performance.
